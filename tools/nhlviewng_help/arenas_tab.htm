Arenas Tab

Just like Players and Teams tab, Arenas tab offers a similar interface with the list on the left and the editing panel on the right:

 

NHLView NG Arenas Tab

 

There is not a whole lot of interesting fields available for editing on the arenas tab. You may edit the name for arenas which have generic names due to licensing issues. You may also modify the capacity and whether the arena uses NHL or International dimensions.

 

Prior to NHL 13, each arena is assigned to a single team so when you modify the properties of the arena, only that team is affected. Since NHL 13, the same arena may be used by multiple teams (for example NHL team and an All-Star team). When you make the change to the arena, all the teams using this arena will be affected.
